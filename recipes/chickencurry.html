Chiecken Curry

Home Page


Description

The aromatic spices and flavors are a delight to the senses! Delicious with fresh naan and basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 3 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • half teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • half teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • half cup coconut milk
  • half lemon, juiced
  • half teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Saut√© onion until lightly browned.
  2. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar, and salt. Continue stirring for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper. Simmer 5 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
