Beef and Guinness Stew

Home Page

Description

This hearty Guinness beef stew is perfect for St Patrick's Day or any cold and blustery day.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Beef
  • Seasonings and herbs
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
    • Fresh garlic
    • White sugar
    • Fresh thyme
  • Can of Guinness beer
  • Vegetables
    • Onions
    • Carrots
    • Celery
  • Home made or bought broth
  • Mashed potatoes

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon
  2. Transfer the bacon to a stew pot, but leave the fat in the skillet.
  3. Cook the beef cubes in the fat.
  4. Place the browned beef in the stew pot.
  5. Cook the onions and garlic in the fat.
  6. Pour the beer in and stir.
  7. Transfer the contents of the skillet to the pot. Add the remaining ingredients.
  8. Simmer until the meat is tender, then bring to a boil.
  9. Serve in bowls or over mashed potatoes.
